About Me

I am a daughter, a sister, and a wife. I love to use my hands to build, to fix, and to create. Besides work, there is not a day where I don't use my hands to accomplish something.

I like to fix things around our house whether it's changing the light fixture to replacing the garbage disposal. I like to show my styles by decorating our house with unique details. I also love to cook and bake! One of the greatest feelings I have is when everyone tells me how much they love my cooking! It puts a smile on my face when I put a smile on their face.
